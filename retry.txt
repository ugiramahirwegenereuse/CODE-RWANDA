CODERWANDA ltd, is software firm located in Kigali-Rwanda. In its department of software engineering, they have proposed to develop a web application that will help transportation system in Rwanda whereby the owner of the car who want to share the ride will post the time he/she is going to start a departure, departure place, the number of available sits and the destination. But before joining a shared ride the client must do the registration and login into the system. Suppose you are a DevOp Engineer in this company and you are tasked to do deliver the registration and login module of the proposed application.
